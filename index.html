Enthusiastic about computational biology to genetic analysis tools and system biology modeling. Has creative and communications skills for problem-solver tasks, leadership and management for building new projects. Also, a (forever) programming and technology student looking for a more equal world.

These attributions guided my interest in developing biological data analysis, gene circuit design tools and scientific educational activities.
